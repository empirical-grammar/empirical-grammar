About Us

Empirical Grammar teaches grammatical concepts to students by giving students essays that have grammatical errors embedded within them. For example, students proofread a 300 word article on United States history that has 30 errors embedded within it. Students proofread the essays and correct the errors. Students are then given personalized homework lessons for the concepts they do not understand.

Teachers can sign up their classes and monitor their students' progress. We currently support middle school students studying English. We cover all of the grammatical concepts from the Common Core State Standards for grades 1-8. We intend to expand our coverage to high school students, college students, ESL students, SAT / ACT students, and foreign language students.

Empirical is a non-profit publisher of interactive learning experiences. Empirical Grammar is the inaugural product of our interactive textbook. The interactive textbook utilizes problem based learning and participation based learning.
